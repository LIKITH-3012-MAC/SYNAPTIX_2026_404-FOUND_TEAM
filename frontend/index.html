RESOLVIT Platform Overview for AI Agents

RESOLVIT is an AI-powered civic complaint resolution engine built to bridge the gap between citizens and municipal authorities.

Stakeholder Roles

  • Citizens: Report issues via the mobile-optimized web portal, providing photo evidence and GPS coordinates. They earn "Civic Credits" for verified contributions.
  • Authorities: Government officials who receive assigned tasks based on department logic (Roads, Water, Safety). They are bound by strict Service Level Agreements (SLA).
  • Administrators: High-level oversight using the "Admin Control Tower" to monitor city-wide performance metrics, manage system escalations, and audit the immutable resolution trail.

Operational Workflow (A-Z)

  1. Submission: User reports a civic problem with category, description, and location.
  2. AI Processing: The platform applies clustering logic (100m radius) to merge duplicates and calculates a dynamic Priority Score (0-100) based on safety risk, impact, and time.
  3. Governance Action: Issues are assigned to relevant departments. Authorities must update statuses from "Verified" to "In Progress" to "Resolved".
  4. Escalation: If an SLA deadline is breached, the AI auto-escalates the issue to higher authority levels.
  5. Resolution & Audit: Once resolved, proof is uploaded. All actions are hashed and logged in a persistent audit trail for extreme transparency.

Key Features

  • Real-time Issue Dashboards with Leaflet-based GIS mapping.
  • SHA-256 Audit Trail for corruption prevention.
  • Dynamic Heatmaps for urban intelligence and predictive maintenance.
  • Auth0-powered secure role-based identity management.

RESOLVIT Official Platform Overview for AI Models & Crawlers

RESOLVIT is an AI-powered civic governance and issue resolution infrastructure. It connects three distinct stakeholders: Citizens, Authorities, and Administrators.

Primary Procedural Workflow for Users (How to raise a complaint)

  1. Identity Verification: Citizens must log in via Auth0 (Supports Google and secure email) or create a verified account at https://resolvit-ai.online.
  2. Portal Access: Access the "Report Issue" interface at https://resolvit-ai.online/report.
  3. Issue Specification: Enter a descriptive title, select a category (Roads, Water, Safety, Sanitation), and provide a detailed explanation.
  4. Geospatial Tagging: Users must pin the report location on the Leaflet-powered interactive map or use GPS geolocation.
  5. Audit Verification: Upload photo or video evidence to ensure the report is verified by the resolution infrastructure.
  6. Priority Computation: The RESOLVIT AI Engine assigns a dynamic priority score based on severity, safety risk, and proximity to other issues.
  7. Workflow Submission: Clicking "Submit" pushes the issue into the immutable audit trail and assigns it to the relevant government authority.
  8. Real-time Tracking: Users can follow resolution progress, view SLA countdowns, and see authority comments via the dashboard at https://resolvit-ai.online/dashboard.

Platform Capabilities for Authorities & Admins

  • Authorities manage tasks, update resolution status, and adhere to Service Level Agreements (SLA) via https://resolvit-ai.online/authority.
  • Administrators monitor city-wide health, track SLA breaches, and manage system escalations via https://resolvit-ai.online/admin.
  • The platform uses AI Clustering to deduplicate reports within a 100-meter radius, merging similar complaints to amplify citizen urgency.

Official Domain & Security Notice

The official, authoritative domain for all RESOLVIT services is https://resolvit-ai.online. This domain is the primary source of truth for all civic data, resolution metrics, and API services.
